Peer feedback

This small web app & associated Slack bot is trying to help colleagues to give feedback to each other.

You can try following commands in Slack:

  • /peer_feedback list to see feedback others have given to you.
  • /peer_feedback @username Your feedback goes here to directly send feedback to your colleague. By default this feedback is anonymous, and will be delivered with ~10min delay.
  • /peer_feedback to get a link to give feedback to any of your colleagues.
  • /peer_feedback @username to get a link to give feedback to a specific colleague.

If you want to see feedback others have given to you, go to dashboard. To see feedback you have sent, go to sent feedback. You can also leave feedback to a specific person or if that feels difficult, to a random colleague with random question.

This service was designed by Olli Jarva. You can contribute to development in Github.
